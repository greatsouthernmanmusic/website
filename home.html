GREAT SOUTHERN MAN

Love an afternoon at the pub? Love a great Aussie Classic tune to sink a pint to? Great Southern Man (Tim Scholl), is putting Aussie Music in your ears 100% of the time. Whether you're a Hoodoo Guru, or a Middle Kid, GSM is all about celebrating our country's great musical history, and showing you a modern Aussie artist or two you will be adding to your favourite playlists. Why? Cause Aussie Music bloody rocks that's why, and we should be pumping it in our pubs and on our airways more to support our artists, just like other countries do.

The idea for Great Southern Man has grown out of gracing stages from Lismore to the Sunshine Coast with some of this country's best musicians, such as the Shannon Beaumont Band, Pink Zinc, Rochelle Lees Band, and the Lydia Storey Trio, as well as his creative contributions to original groups like Headphone Symphony, Arora and Sujitoko.

Great Southern Man offers a range of performance setups, from intimate acoustic sessions to full-scale dance floor loop driven mayhem. The goal is to connect deeply with every audience, from casual cafe gatherings to spirited evenings at the local RSL, and reminding people that Aussie Music is the best in the world.

As this country's music artists continue to evolve and bloom, Great Southern Man invites listeners to experience the overwhelming wealth of absolute bangers this nation has produced over the last 50 years.
