GREAT SOUTHERN MAN

Half Classics, Half Modern Hits, All Aussie

Love an afternoon at the pub? Love a great Aussie classic to sink a pint to? Great Southern Man (Tim Scholl) is putting Aussie music in your ears 100% of the time. Whether you're a Hoodoo Guru or a Middle Kids fan, GSM is about celebrating this country's great musical history and introducing you to a modern Aussie artist or two you'll be adding to your playlists immediately. Why? Because Aussie music rocks, and it deserves more airtime — in our pubs, on our airways, and in our hearts.

The idea for Great Southern Man grew out of years gracing stages from Lismore to the Sunshine Coast alongside some of this country's best musicians — the Shannon Beaumont Band, Pink Zinc, Rochelle Lees Band, and the Lydia Storey Trio — as well as original projects including Headphone Symphony, Arora and Sujitoko.

GSM offers a range of setups — from intimate acoustic sessions to full loop-driven dance floor mayhem. The goal is always the same: remind people that Aussie music is the best in the world.

Guitarniverse

Free guitar lessons. Real technique. All feel.

Guitarniverse is a free library of guitar lessons built around one idea: technique should serve music, not the other way around. Whether you're picking up the guitar for the first time or looking to break through a plateau, these lessons focus on taste, feel, and the things that actually make you sound like a guitarist.
